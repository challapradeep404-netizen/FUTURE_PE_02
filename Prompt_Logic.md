## Prompt Logic – Ad 1 (Instagram Reels: Try‑On Haul)

### Objective
To create a 30–60 second UGC ad script for **Trendsetter**, a clothing boutique in Bengaluru, that feels authentic and natural, like a real customer sharing their shopping experience. The ad should highlight Trendsetter’s diverse clothing range and unique selling points while fitting the Instagram Reels format.

### Context
- **Brand:** Trendsetter boutique, Bengaluru  
- **Target Audience:** Women, men, and children looking for casual, formal, western, and ethnic wear  
- **Platform:** Instagram Reels  
- **Concept:** Try‑On Haul (quick outfit transitions showcasing multiple styles)  

### Design Principles
- Authentic, conversational tone (UGC style)  
- Short-form storytelling (30–60 seconds)  
- Clear flow: Hook → Outfit transitions → CTA  
- Showcase variety (casual, formal, western, ethnic)  
- Emphasize affordability, quality, and handmade designs  
- Visual transitions to keep viewers engaged  

### Structure
1. **Opening Hook** – A scroll-stopping intro (e.g., “Just grabbed these outfits from Trendsetter — let’s try them on!”).  
2. **Scenes** – 2–3 quick outfit transitions showing different clothing types.  
3. **Details** – Highlight Trendsetter’s trend collection, affordable quality, and handmade designs.  
4. **Closing CTA** – End with boutique name + location, encouraging viewers to visit.  

### Example Template
Write a 30–60 second UGC ad script for [Brand], a clothing boutique located in [City].
Details to include: [clothing types, target customers, unique selling points].
Style guidelines: Authentic, natural, like a customer sharing their shopping experience.
Platform: Instagram Reels.
Concept: Try‑On Haul (showing multiple outfits in quick transitions).
Include opening hook, 2–3 short scenes, and a closing call‑to‑action with boutique name and location.


### Outcome
The prompt generated a UGC ad script that:  
- Opens with a relatable hook.
